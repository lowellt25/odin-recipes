Lemon Bars

Ingredients

Crust

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cold butter
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Custard

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest (optional)
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Spray 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Mix 2 cups flour, butter, and 1/2 cup powdered sugar until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Press mixture into baking dish.
  5. Bake in oven until the edges of the crust are golden brown, 18-22 minutes. Cool crust to room temperature.
  6. Beat white sugar, eggs, 1/4 cup flour, lemon juice, baking powder, and lemon zest together in a bowl until light and fluffy; pour onto cooled crust.
  7. Bake in oven until custard is set, 25-28 minutes.
  8. Cool lemon squares completely; dust the top with powdered sugar.
