Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients, making sure butter is softened and eggs are room temperature.
  2. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  6. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake in oven until edges are browned, about 10 minutes.
  9. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Store in airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!
